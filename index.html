Workshop on climate data management, data sharing and exchange 2021

Direction Météorologie Nationale, Morocco and WMO

The climate data management, data sharing and exchange workshop will be organized by the Direction de la Météorologie Nationale of the --, Patras, Morocco, on Thirsday October 28th 2021. The workshop will take place online via the Zoom platform. The link will be sent to the registered participants in due time. The theme of the workshop is

The main purposes of this session are to: - Identify and discuss any specific challenges and issues of the West and Central African regions in relation to data and metadata collection and sharing, - Provide guidance and training on the principles, tools and procedures from both WIGOS and WIS, especially focusing on the latest developments, - Discuss and agree on ways forward regarding improving data quality and data sharing.

Topics

The main topics to be addressed during the workshop are:

  • Introduction to methods and tools for the generation of climate datasets
  • Concept of and introduction to computing World Weather Records,
  • Concept of and introduction to computing climatological standard normals for 1991-2020,
  • Concept of and introduction to computing National Climate Monitoring Products,
  • Quality control and Homogenization of climate data time-series, homogenization methods,
  • Use of WIS to share climate data and products,
  • The WIGOS Data Quality Monitoring System (WDQMS) functions and webtool, Metadata,
  • Management of WIGOS metadata with OSCAR/Surface and implementation of WIGOS Station Identifiers.
